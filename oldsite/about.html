About Us

Hanna Stables is amongst the oldest riding establishments in Belize. In the early 1900's Migrant Lebanese came to this part of the World and settled in Cayo, starting one of the first cattle and horse farms in the area. The stables opened in 1987 for guests at the farm and has never looked back.

We arrange weekends away as small groups to cut the costs of travel, and to ensure that everyone gets a trustworthy life adventure.

Although we're based in the west, we don't stay on our own turf: past riding weekends have included trips to un-excavated Mayan Sites few miles away. When we're not riding, we often go cave tubing, biking or just a jungle stroll for birding and small mammal( sight seeing).

As Ted always say...

"Live life to the fullest take advantage of all adventures and fun!"
