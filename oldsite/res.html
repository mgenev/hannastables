Home Grown Food

Enjoy farm style meals freshly prepared by our competent cooks simple but wholesome meals include ethically raised meat from our very own cattle herd and goat flock, milk yoghurts and cheeses made on the farm from our friendly dairy herd, and not to exclude the vegetable and fruit lovers we also have a whole range of organically grown produce such as tomatoes, potatoes, avocados mangoes sour sop, maize, beans, coconuts, and plantain.

All our meal are served with freshly squeezed fruit juice based on your choice. We are known for the best local meals with mouth watering desirts.

In keeping with our long standing commitment to try and maintain a minimal impact on our world’s resources we only provide meals that include locally grown and in season vegetables and fruits. We care for the environment hence all our waste products are recycle.

On the farm there are many wooded areas and riparian zones maintained as wildlife reserves for a host of flora and fauna such as armadillos, peccaries, foxes, and ocelot and a small organic coffee plantation.
