Nabitunich-The Stone Cottages

Welcome to Nabitunich, "The Little Stone Cottage" in Maya, a world nestled at the foot of the great Mayan temple of XUNANTUNICH, which means 'The Lady of Stone'.

The Stone Cottage is located on the Western Highway, 71 miles from Belize City and 3 miles from the Guatemalan border. The cottages are a ten minute walk from the entrance. Each cottage has two bedrooms, which can accommodate two or three people.

Exotic gardens surround the cottages, which provide the perfect setting to witness a spectacular sunset behind Xunantunich. The resort nestles in the centre of a 400-acre farm; which has belonged to the Juan family for several generations. On the farm there are many wooded areas and a small coffee plantation down beside the Mopan River, which flows for a mile through the farm.

Apart from the delightful trails for those interested in birding or simply watching nature; the Mopan River is a joy just to watch, as it alternates between tumbling little waterfalls and more leisurely gliding passages on its way to join the Macal River a few miles downstream. Together they form the Belize Old River, which snakes through villages with names like Esperanza (Hope) and Young Girl, on its way to the Caribbean Sea.

For those who enjoy a little more adventure on the water, there is canoeing, tubing, swimming and fishing. You can combine tubing with a day on the river, watching Iguana lie on the branches of great trees sunning themselves or the myriad birds and flowers one sees along the river banks.

After a day thus described, it is good to come home. For that is what we try to offer you at the Stone Cottages. A home from home experience. Our accommodations and our food are both clean and wholesome. How does this evening menu tempt you? Fruit freshly picked from the orchards, wholesome soup, a main course of baked chicken or roast beef all served with a variety of vegetables organically grown here on the farm, green salad and of course refreshing fruit juice.

Activities are arranged to suit your itinerary, ranging from horse riding, swimming, birding or just taking a leisurely walk on one of our nature trails. We have been providing a "Home Away From Home" holiday to guests from around the world for15 years, so come and let us be your hosts at Nabtiunich, and make your trip to Belize an unforgettable experience.
